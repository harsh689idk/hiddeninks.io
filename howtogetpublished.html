Get Published on hiddenink.in

HiddenInks.io empowers emerging authors by providing a modern platform to showcase your work. Submit your manuscript by following the process below.

1. Prepare Your Manuscript

Ensure your book is professionally edited and formatted.

2. Write a Synopsis

Craft a compelling summary (up to 200 words) that intrigues readers.

3. Submit Your Book

Email your manuscript and synopsis to inkshidden@gmail.com with the subject "Book Submission - [Your Title]".

4. Review Process

Our editorial team will review your submission and contact you within 2-4 weeks.
